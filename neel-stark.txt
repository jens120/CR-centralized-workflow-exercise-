Hi there, Let me tell you about the subtle art of making kanda poha.

Ingredients:
* rice flakes
* groundnut oil
* green chillies
* mustard seeds
* cumin seeds
* curry leaves
* asafoetida
* turmeric powder
* sugar

Instructions:
* Rinse 1 cup rice flakes (per person) with water and let it remain soaked for 2 min. 
 Then strain away the water and spread them out on a plate.
